# Product Overview

Portal S4A is a comprehensive corporate intranet system built for business management and internal operations.

## Core Features

- **Dashboard**: Real-time statistics and system overview
- **HR Management**: Employee management, occurrences, hierarchies, teams, and calendar
- **CRM System**: Client portfolio, opportunities (Kanban), orders, products, inventory, billing, and post-sales
- **Intranet**: Internal chat, notes, and useful links
- **Reports**: Power BI integration for business intelligence
- **Settings**: User management, themes, system configuration, and apps management
- **Authentication**: Role-based access control with granular permissions

## Business Modules

1. **HR (Human Resources)**: Complete employee lifecycle management
2. **CRM (Customer Relationship Management)**: Sales pipeline and client management
3. **Intranet**: Internal communication and knowledge sharing
4. **Reports**: Data visualization and business analytics
5. **Settings**: System administration and configuration

## Target Users

- HR managers and staff
- Sales teams and managers
- System administrators
- General employees (intranet access)
- Management (reports and analytics)

## Key Value Propositions

- Centralized business operations platform
- Real-time collaboration and communication
- Comprehensive reporting and analytics
- Customizable themes and user experience
- Scalable role-based permission system
